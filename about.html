About
VendorVerify

We exist to give every Indian business the same quality of vendor intelligence that global corporations take for granted — delivered with speed, precision, and complete confidentiality.

Our Mission

Helping Businesses Make
Safer Vendor Decisions

VendorVerify was founded on a straightforward premise: the risk of onboarding an unverified vendor is almost always greater than the cost of verifying them properly.

Every year, Indian businesses of all sizes suffer preventable losses from vendor fraud, GST compliance failures, supply chain disruptions, and procurement irregularities. The common thread is simple — insufficient vendor verification at the point of onboarding.

VendorVerify provides the structured risk intelligence framework to prevent these outcomes — combining official database verification, legal record searches, and analyst-driven assessment into clear, actionable reports that procurement teams and compliance departments can act on with confidence.
