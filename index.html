Risk Awareness

How Businesses Lose Money to Vendors

Unverified suppliers can create direct financial losses, legal exposure, and operational disruption. These risks are preventable when due diligence happens before payment release.

Fake Supplier Identity

A vendor shares forged registration details, receives an advance, and disappears before delivery.

Advance Payment Fraud

Procurement teams are pressured into urgent advance transfers with no verifiable operational proof.

Shell Company Exposure

A newly formed entity with no real business footprint is used to route funds from high-value transactions.

Hidden Legal & Financial Distress

Litigation-heavy or financially unstable suppliers fail mid-contract, causing delayed orders and recovery losses.

VendorVerify helps businesses verify vendors BEFORE making payments so finance and procurement teams can avoid preventable losses.
