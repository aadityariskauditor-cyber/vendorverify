Company overview

About VendorVerify

VendorVerify is a vendor risk intelligence service designed to help businesses evaluate supplier credibility before entering commercial transactions.

What the service evaluates

The service focuses on identifying operational, legal, financial, and governance risk indicators associated with new or unfamiliar vendors.

How it reduces risk

VendorVerify helps organizations reduce exposure to supplier fraud, payment default, and operational misrepresentation by providing structured vendor verification and due diligence reports.

Target clients include

  • MSMEs
  • Procurement teams
  • Manufacturers
  • Importers and traders
  • Corporate compliance teams
