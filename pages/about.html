About VendorVerify

VendorVerify was built to streamline vendor due diligence and give teams confidence in every supplier relationship.

About the Founder

Aditya Chaudhary
Vendor Risk & Advance Payment Due Diligence Consultant

Aditya Chaudhary works in the field of MSME consulting and vendor risk advisory. His work focuses on helping businesses verify suppliers, reduce fraud risk, and structure safer commercial transactions when onboarding new vendors.

Through structured vendor due diligence, he supports manufacturing businesses, procurement teams, and traders in identifying risk signals before advance payments are released to new suppliers.

Operating Region

Pan-India remote verification.

Physical verification services are subject to availability and are primarily conducted across Delhi NCR and nearby industrial regions, including Haryana, Punjab, Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh, Uttarakhand, Himachal Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, and Gujarat.
