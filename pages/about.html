Our Verification Methodology

VendorVerify follows a layered methodology that starts with identity validation and progressively deepens into compliance, litigation, financial behaviour, and operational verification. Every case is reviewed by analysts who convert raw data into a practical risk recommendation for procurement and finance teams.

Our process blends data-backed checks with contextual assessment so clients can decide whether to proceed, pause, or request additional safeguards before release of funds.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make supplier onboarding safer for Indian and global businesses by reducing preventable fraud losses and bringing clarity to high-value vendor decisions.
