What Is Included in Vendor Verification

Vendor verification is built to uncover hidden supplier risk before your business shares advance payments or signs high-value contracts. The process combines identity checks, compliance screening, and practical risk interpretation.

FLAGO Vendor Risk Assessment Methodology

VendorVerify applies a structured FLAGO methodology to investigate each vendor profile across five core risk dimensions.

  • F – Financial Stability
  • L – Legal & Litigation Review
  • A – Authenticity of Registration
  • G – Governance & Promoter Background
  • O – Operational Capability

The due diligence review generates a Vendor Risk Score (0–100) and assigns a risk classification:

  • Low Risk
  • Moderate Risk
  • High Risk
  • Critical Risk
