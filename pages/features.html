Vendor Verification Service Scope

Our analysts conduct independent vendor verification using multiple public and regulatory data sources including GSTN, MCA filings, and compliance records.

FLAGO Vendor Risk Assessment Methodology

VendorVerify applies a structured FLAGO methodology to investigate each vendor profile across five core risk dimensions.

  • F – Financial Stability
  • L – Legal & Litigation Review
  • A – Authenticity of Registration
  • G – Governance & Promoter Background
  • O – Operational Capability

The due diligence review generates a Vendor Risk Score (0–100) and assigns a risk classification:

  • Low Risk
  • Moderate Risk
  • High Risk
  • Critical Risk

Vendor Risk Assessment Report

Clients typically receive:

  • Vendor Verification Summary
  • Vendor Risk Score
  • Risk Classification
  • Red Flag Indicators
  • Transaction Safeguard Recommendations

Optional report additions:

  • verification screenshots
  • document authenticity analysis
  • facility verification evidence

Future Platform Vision

VendorVerify is building a vendor risk intelligence platform that will help businesses track supplier compliance signals and verification data over time. Today, our core offering remains analyst-led vendor verification and supplier due diligence.
