Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 14/03/2026

VendorVerify is committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of information provided by users of its website and services.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected.

1. Information Collection

VendorVerify may collect the following types of information:

  • user contact information (name, email, phone number)
  • company or vendor details submitted for verification
  • communication records between clients and VendorVerify
  • payment and transaction information processed via payment providers

VendorVerify does not store full payment card details on its servers.

2. Purpose of Processing

2.1 Processing Purposes

Information is collected for the following purposes:

  • performing vendor risk analysis
  • delivering investigative reports
  • processing payments
  • communicating with clients regarding services
  • improving service quality

2.2 Service Improvement and Communication

Information may also be used to communicate with clients regarding services, respond to support inquiries, and improve service quality.

3. Data Sharing

3.1 Sharing Limitations

VendorVerify does not sell or rent personal information to third parties.

Information may be shared only when necessary for:

  • regulatory compliance
  • lawful investigations
  • service delivery requirements

3.2 Third-Party Payment Processing

Payments may be processed through third-party payment service providers such as:

  • Razorpay
  • Stripe

VendorVerify does not control how such providers process payment data.

4. Data Security

4.1 Security Safeguards

VendorVerify implements reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information from unauthorized access or misuse.

4.2 Security Limitation Notice

However, no internet transmission system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

5. Data Retention and User Rights

5.1 Data Retention

Information is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill service obligations or comply with legal requirements.

5.2 User Rights and Contact

Users may request access to or deletion of their personal information by contacting VendorVerify.
