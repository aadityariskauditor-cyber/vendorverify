Refund Policy

Last Updated: 14/03/2026

VendorVerify provides investigative vendor verification and risk intelligence services involving research, analysis, and investigative assessment.

Due to the nature of these services, refunds are subject to the following conditions.

1. Policy Introduction

Refunds may be issued only if the verification process has not yet commenced.

A verification process is considered commenced once research, analysis, or investigation of the vendor has begun.

For refund determination, investigative activity performed after order confirmation constitutes partial service delivery once analysis begins.

2. Eligibility Framework

2.1 Eligible Circumstances

Refunds will not be issued where:

  • the verification process has started
  • partial or full analytical work has been completed
  • a report has been delivered
  • incorrect or misleading information was provided by the client
  • the client chooses not to use the delivered report

2.2 Non-Eligible Circumstances

If a refund request is approved, it will be processed within 5–7 business days using the original payment method.

Refund processing times may vary depending on banking and payment gateway procedures.

3. Request Process

3.1 Submission Requirements

If VendorVerify is unable to deliver a report due to internal service limitations, system errors, or operational failure, VendorVerify may issue a partial or full refund at its discretion.

3.2 Review Timeline

Refund requests must be submitted within 24 hours of placing the order.

4. Refund Execution

4.1 Approved Refund Method

Requests should be sent to:

Email: info@vendorverify.in

4.2 Dispute Handling

VendorVerify will review requests based on service status, work completed, and the circumstances of each case.

5. Contact Information

For additional refund support or policy clarification, contact:

Email: info@vendorverify.in
